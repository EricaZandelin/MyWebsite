Lionfly Secrets

Lionfly Secrets is my band where I am a songwriter/producer/singer and second guitarist. If you are interested in listening to the music I am doing, you can have a lookthrough here. But who are Lionfly? Formed in 2015 by Erica Zandelin and Sebastian Fernstedt, Lionfly Secrets took the south of Sweden by storm with their unique blend of indie, folk, rock and electronica. After some lineup changes, including adding Johannes Renklint on drums, Renzo Jaldin on bass in 2016, they had a brief hiatus, returning victoriously in 2020 with the rock banger ‘Down The Lines’, available on all streaming platforms. Now they’re in the studio, cooking up some more magic.
