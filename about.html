Who am I?

With knowledge and passion for both the music industry and web programming/development, I am now on the market looking for a new opportunity. With experience from jobs in a variety of sectors, I can bring a broad range of skills to the table. But who am I? I am a 28 year old who recently moved back to my first home, Malmö, after an upbringing in the forests of Småland followed by a year abroad in London. In my spare time you can find me out and about with my dog, working out, rehearsing or recording with my band in the studio, or playing games with friends. I’ve dedicated a lot of my time to music, both as an artist, songwriter, producer but also on the event and management end. In a nutshell, I am a person with energy who loves to make things happen. I give a project my all and enjoy the hard work that goes into building something and learning along the way. Last year I discovered a coding bootcamp which caught my attention, and here I am absolutely loving it. I am very excited to continue down this road and learn more about the web programming and development world. I am hoping you would be the one to take the chance on someone who is relatively new to the branch and ready to develop within a company. I am looking forward hearing from you, and don’t forget to check out my CV
